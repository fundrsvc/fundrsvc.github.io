If you exceed your Paid plan limits, you will be prompted to upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional usage. You can also contact our customer support team to discuss options to increase your limits. They will be able to offer you the best solution based on your specific needs and usage. Keep in mind that regularly exceeding your usage limits may indicate that it's time to upgrade to a higher plan that better fits your needs.

Our team is happy to help you determine the best plan for your specific needs. You can also take advantage of our free trial to test out the features and capabilities of our platform before committing to a paid plan. Additionally, you can always upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time to best fit your current needs. We provide transparent pricing and flexible options to suit your budget and usage.

No, there are no long-term commitments or cancellation fees with FundrsVC. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time.

Simply log in to your account and navigate the billing section to cancel your monthly subscription. From there, you will be able to cancel your subscription. If you have any issues canceling, please contact our customer support team, who will be happy to assist you with the cancellation process.

When you upgrade or downgrade a plan mid-period, the changes will take effect immediately and reflect on your next billing cycle. The charges will be prorated based on the number of days remaining in the current billing period. Any unused features or quotas on the previous plan will not be carried over to the new plan. Additionally, any changes made to the plan will be reflected in your account immediately, so you can start using the new features or increased limits immediately.
